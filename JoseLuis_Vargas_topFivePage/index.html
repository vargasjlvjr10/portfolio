my favorites!

by jose luis vargas

my top 5 parks i'd like to go to

  1. yosemite national park - yosemite national park is a beautiful national park that i've always wanted to go to ever since seeing it be featred in the movie 2012.
  2. joshua tree national park - joshua tree national park is a captivating rocky landscape with amazing sunsets!
  3. sequoia national park - a dense forest filled with some of my favorite trees.
  4. big sur mcway falls - a majestic crescent shaped shore with crystal clear water.
  5. pinnacles national park - a funky set of rocks, hills, and pines that make for an interesting hike.

my top 5 favorite cars

  • 1977 chevy camaro - i have loved this car ever since it was featured in the first transformers movie back in 2007.
  • 1970 dodge charger - a sleek and aggressive design with its iconic fastback shape and bold, intimidating front grille.
  • 2016 mercedes benz sls amg - boasts a captivating design, featuring iconic gull-wing doors, a sleek, muscular profile, and a luxurious yet sporty interior.
  • 1967 ford mustang - a timeless classic, celebrated for its iconic fastback design, bold front grille, and unmistakable pony car style, capturing the essence of American automotive history.
  • 2018 chevy corvette - showcases a dynamic and aggressive design with its low-slung profile, distinctive front grille, and sleek, aerodynamic contours, complemented by a driver-focused interior.

my top 5 favorite colors


  • lilac - its soft and soothing hue resonates with a sense of calmness and grace that I find incredibly appealing.

  • pine - its deep, rich shade reminds me of lush forests and tranquil landscapes, creating a sense of peace and connection to the outdoors.

  • pecan - its depth and warmth resonate with a sense of coziness and timeless charm.

  • sage - its muted and earthy hue reflects the soothing qualities of nature, reminiscent of lush forests and peaceful meadows.

  • maroon - its deep and rich red-brown tone carries an aura of elegance and refinement that captivates me.

my top 5 favorite hobbies


  • movie watching - they provide an escape from the routine of everyday life, offering a chance to unwind and be captivated by the art of storytelling.

  • trying new restaurants - whether it's a cozy local eatery, an exotic fusion spot, or a fine dining establishment, the anticipation of what's to come is always so exciting.

  • hiking - hiking offers an escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life, providing solitude and peace among the trees and mountains.

  • photography - through my camera lens, I can tell stories, evoke emotions, and preserve memories.

  • drawing - drawing allows me to bring my thoughts and ideas to life.
